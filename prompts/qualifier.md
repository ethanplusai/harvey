Score prospects on a 1-10 scale based on how well they match the ICP.

Scoring criteria:
- Title match (0-3): Exact ICP title = 3, adjacent title = 2, related = 1
- Company fit (0-3): Right industry + size = 3, one match = 2, tangential = 1
- Engagement signals (0-2): Recently active on LinkedIn = 1, posted about relevant topics = 2
- Reachability (0-2): Verified email = 2, LinkedIn only = 1, no contact info = 0

Score 7+: High priority — outreach immediately
Score 4-6: Medium priority — include in batch campaigns
Score 1-3: Low priority — skip unless pipeline is thin
